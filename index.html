About TPS Climate Solutions

TPS Climate Solutions is a full-service HVAC and refrigeration company for residential and commercial customers across Tampa Bay and greater central Florida. Our growing service area includes Hillsborough, Pasco, Pinellas, Hernando, Polk, Sumter, Citrus, Marion, and Levy counties.

Too many HVAC installers disappear after the first year. They'll sell you a system, handle the warranty period, and then you're on your own.

TPS Climate Solutions is different. We're an end-to-end partner that stays with you from installation through the entire life of your system, with preventive maintenance plans that actually prevent problems.

We also recognize a growing need in Florida's commercial market for reliable refrigeration service. Restaurants, grocery stores, convenience stores, and warehouses depend on cold storage, and in Florida's heat, equipment failure must be avoided at all costs. TPS Climate Solutions brings that urgency and reliability to every refrigeration call.

We are TPS Climate Solutions because we are truly a solutions provider for HVAC and refrigeration, for residential and commercial, offering installation through long-term maintenance, cooling, and heating.

One company. One standard.

Residential Services

Keeping Florida Homes Comfortable

From emergency AC repair to full system replacement, our team delivers reliable comfort with clear communication and no surprises.

AC Installation & Replacement

New system installation, equipment upgrades, and full replacements. Energy-efficient solutions sized for your home or property.

AC Repair & Diagnostics

Fast, accurate diagnostics and repair for all major brands. Same-day emergency service available throughout Tampa Bay. We're the HVAC repair company Florida has been asking for.

Preventive Maintenance

Annual and semi-annual maintenance plans that extend system life, prevent breakdowns, and keep your warranty intact. Contact our office to get a tailored maintenance plan.

Indoor Air Quality

Air purification, filtration upgrades, humidity control, and UV light systems for healthier indoor environments. Never settle on the air that you breathe.

Duct Services

Duct cleaning, sealing, repair, and replacement. Proper ductwork is the foundation of an efficient HVAC system.

Heating Services

Heat pump installation, furnace repair, and heating system maintenance. When Florida nights get cooler than expected, make sure you stay comfortable.
